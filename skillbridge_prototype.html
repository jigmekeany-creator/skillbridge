SkillBridge Bhutan

Connecting Youth Skills with Local Opportunities

The Problem

Many young people in Bhutan have valuable skills such as graphic design, tutoring, coding, photography and video editing, but struggle to find opportunities to use them.

The Solution

SkillBridge connects skilled youth with people or businesses that need help with small jobs. It helps students gain experience while helping communities access local talent.

Example Skills

Graphic Design
Logos, posters, social media posts
Tutoring
Math, English, Science
Photography
Events and editing
Tech Help
Website help, device setup

Example Task

  • Task: Design a Poster
  • Location: Thimphu
  • Budget: Nu. 500
  • Contact: example@email.com

Future Vision

Future features may include verified student profiles, ratings and reviews, secure payments, and partnerships with schools and businesses.
